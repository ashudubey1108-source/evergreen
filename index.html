Everything you need to know before your move

FAQs

Got Questions?

We've answered the most common questions our customers ask.

How far in advance should I book my move? ▼
We recommend booking at least 7–10 days in advance for local moves and 15–20 days for inter-city moves. During peak season (April-June, Oct-Nov), book earlier to secure your preferred date.
Are my goods insured during transit? ▼
Yes! All our moves include basic transit insurance. We also offer comprehensive declared-value insurance for high-value items. Our team will explain all insurance options during your pre-move survey.
Do you provide packing materials? ▼
Absolutely. We use high-quality packing materials including 3-ply corrugated boxes, bubble wrap, foam sheets, stretch wrap, and custom wooden crates for fragile and high-value items. All materials are included in our package pricing.
Can I track my goods during transit? ▼
Yes! All our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking systems. You'll receive a tracking link via WhatsApp once your goods are loaded. You can track your shipment in real-time from anywhere.
What items are not allowed in the move? ▼
We cannot transport hazardous materials, flammable liquids, gas cylinders (full), perishable food items, illegal items, or live plants in some states. Our team will provide a complete list during your survey.
How is the final price calculated? ▼
Pricing is based on the volume/weight of goods, distance between origin and destination, floor number and elevator availability, type of items (fragile, heavy machinery), and add-on services chosen. We provide a transparent, itemized quote after the free pre-move survey.
Do you offer storage facilities? ▼
Yes. We have secure, climate-controlled warehouse facilities in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Pune. Short-term (daily) and long-term (monthly) storage options are available at competitive rates.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept cash, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), cheque, and all major credit/debit cards. Typically, 30-50% advance is required at booking and the balance upon delivery.
Can I cancel or reschedule my move? ▼
Yes. Cancellations or rescheduling made more than 48 hours before the move are free of charge. Changes within 48 hours may incur a nominal fee. Please contact your move manager immediately if you need to make changes.
